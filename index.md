<h1 align="center">Discord IM Online Tracker for Second Life</h1>

The Truth &amp; Beauty Lab creates and maintains projects to monitor the online status
of Second Life avatars and send live notifications of login/logoff either via Instant
Messages (IM) or messages to a configured Discord channel.

### In-World Scripted Object

`Discord IM Online Tracker` is a scripted prim frame that indicates the online status of a configured avatar.
The in-world object sends the owner an IM when the target avatar logs in or logs off. The frame displays the avatar's profile picture and the border color indicates online status.

The object can be configured to also send status messages to a Discord channel.
